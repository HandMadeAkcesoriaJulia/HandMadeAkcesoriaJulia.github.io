About me

I am Yulia and I'm a mother of 2 kids. My daughter inspired me to create accesories, because I wanted her to be the most beautiful. My friends and family liked them, so I decided to make accesories for other kids too. Later I started making accesories for adults. I always had the desire to create something beautiful. I finished the art school and even now I draw pictures for myself. But most of all my creativity is manifested in my accesories. Now I have a lot of customers of any age. Every wedding photo with a happy bride with my accesories makes me happy too.
